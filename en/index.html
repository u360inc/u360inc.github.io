What We Do

Our main expertise is problem solving and execution support in business development.

事業開発コンサルティング

We offer consulting service to help clients create business strategies for new business, products, and services, and execute the strategies with project management and talent management expertise.

人材育成ファシリテーター
[ Seminars, Workshops, OJT ]

We offer a range of learning opportunities through seminars, workshops and OJT programs for

  • Business skills such as intelligence gathering, problem solving and marketing
  • Customer interview based on Lean Startup
  • Action learning through planning and execution of new product/service launch and PR strategy

[ Internal Training Program Development ]

We help clients define core competencies, shape internal training programs, including content creation for training programs and tuning up operation manuals.

コンサル転職支援

異業種からコンサルティング業界への転職、女性のコンサルティング業界への転職を中心に転職人材紹介を行います。

Marketing and PR Support

新商品・サービスのロンチのPR活動の企画検討や自社広報部門の立ち上げ、プレスリリースの作成代行など広報・PR機能の強化に取り組みます。
